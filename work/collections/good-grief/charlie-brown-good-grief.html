Good Grief, Charlie Brown!

A Peanuts-sized poetry collection

Good grief, Charlie Brown!

an anagrammatic poem

be worried
feel fear for
a fine filigree nerd
windbag weirdo
a Faberge egghead
bowlegged
bedraggled
a bodger
owner of a brain boggling
beagle

a gloried ace
arrowing in
for a brioche
a beer
an echo of war
in a borrowed Fedora

danger!
danger!
a dog
or
a horrific fiend
a howling ego

now, now
no bawling
hide in a barn
or a barge
crawl in a hedgerow

oh no!
down dog, down
