A Peanuts-sized poetry collection

Lunchtime in an American Schoolyard


a boy in a yellow sweater
opens a brown paper bag

the smell of peanut butter
makes him think of home

he floats out of the playground
holds himself quietly above

all the giddy little riots
as he waits for the bell

to signal another ceasefire
