Good Grief, Charlie Brown!

A Peanuts-sized poetry collection

Charlie Brown, you blockhead!

an anagrammatic poem

once, when you ran around
lonely under a black cloud,
you and your ulcer
heading for a coronary,
I only knew you were
an unlucky curio,
like a crinkled nickel

now I am older,
you are here
all burrowed down,
like when your
black-eared hound
needed to hide in your bed,
red baron inbound
