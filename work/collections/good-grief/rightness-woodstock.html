Good Grief, Charlie Brown!

A Peanuts-sized poetry collection

The enduring rightness of Woodstock


each time you take flight you genuinely reiterate your intention to fly upright
but somehow the appeal of the conventional mode
lacks the persistence of, say, gravity
& unable to hold yourself properly perpendicular
you find yourself in this unusual situation
the blood rushing downward toward the top of your bright head
still making forward progress despite what they all said
